## Browser Extension

### Browsec

[Browsec](https://browsec.com/) is a browser extension that provides a simple and user-friendly way to enhance your online privacy and security. Here are some key features:

- **Virtual Locations**

- **Secure Browsing**

- **Easy to Use**

- **Ad Blocking**

- **No Registration Required**
